Practice Areas

Labor Law

We provide comprehensive labor counseling to generate positive relationships and reduce labor risks. Our services include due diligence, complex litigation, contracts, terminations, day-to-day management consulting, and labor considerations in business acquisitions.

View more

We provide constant advice and support to our clients and their work teams to generate good relations with their workers, reduce the risks of their activity, and protect their interests, being able to contribute our knowledge in matters such as labor due diligence, litigation of high amount and complexity, relations with the competent authority, drafting of contracts, settlements, daily advice to management, acquisition of companies and their labor implications.

Litigation

We have vast experience in litigation and trials in different economic sectors. Our services include special cases, civil and commercial disputes, litigation in various jurisdictions, drafting of appeals, and complex litigation in the Courts of Appeals and the Supreme Court.

View more

Given the diverse sectors of the economy in which our clients participate, we have been able to participate and have extensive experience in litigation and trials of all types, special trials, civil and commercial disputes, litigation in all types of venues, drafting of appeals, complex litigation both in Courts of Appeals and before the Supreme Court.

Corporate

We provide comprehensive corporate advice on incorporation of companies, foreign equity, due diligence, share purchase and sale, management of shareholders' books and commercial contracts, and support our clients in all their operations.

View more

We offer our clients comprehensive services to support all stages of their operations. From the incorporation of companies and formalization of foreign capital to due diligence, purchase, and sale of shares, administration of shareholders' books, and drafting of commercial contracts, we provide constant advice and support. We have extensive experience and knowledge in the corporate field, guaranteeing effective legal solutions and protection of our client's interests in an increasingly challenging and competitive business environment.

Banking and financing

We advise our clients on debt restructuring, and represent our clients before banking committees. We are experts in external taxation, litigation, and internal controls. We protect financial interests with strategic and effective solutions.

View more

Our practice has allowed us to provide specialized advice in debt restructuring processes, representing the interests of our clients before risk committees in different banking institutions. Our lawyers have extensive experience in external banking prosecution services, both in litigation and in the review of internal controls, following the legal and specific criteria of each institution. Our practice is focused on guaranteeing the protection of our client's financial interests and providing effective and strategic solutions.

Consumer Law

Experience in consumer law. Legal analysis of advertising, labels, and products. Legal representation in consumer and institutional actions.

View more

We have extensive experience in consumer law, working together with our clients and their marketing managers, to perform a legal analysis of their advertising, labels, and products, as well as representing our clients in legal actions brought by individuals or the National Consumer Service.

Tax law

We review and advise on tax compliance, identifying risks and contingencies. We support inheritance proceedings and represent in administrative and judicial audits before the Internal Revenue Service and the Tax and Customs Courts (TTA).

View more

We review tax compliance and advise our clients in the determination of their tax obligations. We evaluate the tax situation, providing and preparing various compliance alternatives that generate optimal tax compliance, and identifying potential risks and tax contingencies that may affect both the company and the personal assets of its partners or shareholders.

We also provide advice on inheritance processes, to consolidate family businesses and defend our clients in administrative auditing processes before the Internal Revenue Service, and in judicial proceedings before the Tax and Customs Courts (TTA).

Team
